### 🏗️ Executive Brief: Phishing Infiltration
A governance and culture risk. The market views phishing as a failure of employee training and internal discipline.

**72-Hour Impact Projections:**
* **Mandatory Executive Security Audit:** (-1% Stock, -2 NPS) Transparently acknowledging the slip-up and forcing all staff to retake training. Seen as a highly responsible, proactive measure.
* **Quietly Terminate Responsible Employee:** (-0.5% Stock, -5 NPS) Swift internal action keeps it out of the press, but risks an internal culture of fear and potential whistleblower leaks.
* **Hire Expensive External Consultants:** (-4% Stock, -8 NPS) Signals panic to investors. Admitting the internal team cannot handle basic hygiene hurts short-term stock value.
* **Ignore / Cover It Up:** (-8% Stock, -25 NPS) If it leaks to the press that executives knew about a compromised account and did nothing, the brand takes a massive hit.
