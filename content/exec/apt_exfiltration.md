### 🕵️ Executive Brief: A.P.T. Data Exfiltration
A massive regulatory and brand equity crisis. The public has learned that their most sensitive data has been stolen over a period of months due to negligence.

**72-Hour Impact Projections:**
* **Full GDPR/CCPA Disclosure & Free Identity Theft Protection:** (-18% Stock, -25 NPS) Expensive, legally mandated compliance. The stock drops due to the cost of fines and credit monitoring, but it is the industry standard response.
* **Downplay the Sensitivity of the Stolen Data:** (-10% Stock, -70 NPS) Claiming "only basic info was taken" to calm investors backfires spectacularly when hackers release the unencrypted credit cards online.
* **Resignation of the CEO and CISO:** (-25% Stock, -10 NPS) Offering up the executive team as a sacrificial lamb. Creates massive leadership instability (tanking the stock) but shows the public that accountability exists.
