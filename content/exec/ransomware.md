### 🔒 Executive Brief: Ransomware (The Operational Blackout)
A business continuity crisis resulting in an immediate cessation of cash flow and a catastrophic drop in customer trust.

**72-Hour Impact Projections:**
*   **Wipe systems and restore from off-site backups:** Causes a 12% stock drop and a 35-point NPS loss, garnering respect for taking the hard path.
*   **Partial restore and isolation of network nodes:** Causes an 18% stock drop and a 50-point NPS loss due to frustration with system glitches.
*   **Pay the ransom and wait for the decryption key:** Causes an 8% stock drop and a 20-point NPS loss, bringing relief but ethical backlash.
*   **Attempt to manually decrypt files without a key:** Causes a 30% stock drop and a 65-point NPS loss as the move is perceived as delusional.
*   **Restart systems without a deep clean:** Causes a 50% stock drop and an 85-point NPS loss, leading to catastrophic brand death.
