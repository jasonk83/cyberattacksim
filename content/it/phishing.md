### 🏗️ IT System Impact: The Phishing Foothold

**Recovery Timelines by Decision:**
*   **Full credential reset & system-wide password change:** Requires an 8-hour recovery time[cite: 1].
*   **Targeted reset of compromised account & enhanced monitoring:** Requires a 2-hour recovery time[cite: 1].
*   **Forensic investigation and log auditing (Watch & Wait):** Requires a 48-hour recovery time[cite: 1].
*   **Basic user training and "reminder" email:** Requires an ongoing recovery time[cite: 1].
*   **No action taken; system continues as normal:** Results in an indefinite recovery time[cite: 1].
