### 💾 IT System Impact: Data Exfiltration (Integrity Crisis)
A cold crisis where systems remain up, but IT shifts to egress filtering and heavy log analysis to determine what was stolen.

**Recovery Timelines by Decision:**
*   **Full audit, disclosure, and credit monitoring setup:** Requires a 72-hour recovery time.
*   **Segmented audit and narrow, targeted disclosure:** Requires a 1-week recovery time.
*   **Delay disclosure to perform an in-depth forensic study:** Requires a 30-day recovery time.
*   **Stealth patching and public silence:** Recovery time is N/A[.
*   **Delete system logs to obscure the extent of the theft:** Requires years of recovery time.
