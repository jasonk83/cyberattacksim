### 🔒 IT System Impact: Ransomware (Operational Blackout)
The network is considered toxic, causing a total operational shutdown where hardware must be physically isolated to stop the spread[cite: 1].

**Recovery Timelines by Decision:**
*   **Wipe all systems and restore from clean, off-site backups:** Requires a 4-day recovery time[cite: 1].
*   **Partial restore and isolation of infected network nodes:** Requires a 2-week recovery time[cite: 1].
*   **Pay the ransom and wait for the decryption key:** Requires a 72-hour recovery time[cite: 1].
*   **Attempt to manually decrypt files without a key:** Requires a 6-month recovery time[cite: 1].
*   **Restart systems without a deep clean:** Results in a permanent loss[cite: 1].
